Animal welfare activists argue that farm animals should have space to move.
They point to studies showing that overcrowded barns cause stress.
Many campaigns focus on ending the use of battery cages for hens.
Supporters encourage farmers to adopt cage-free systems.
They also push for laws that mandate humane treatment.
Educational outreach informs consumers about these issues.
Volunteers visit schools to talk about compassion toward animals.
Social media is used to share footage from factory farms.
The movement grows as more people choose ethical products.
Advocates believe that every animal deserves kindness.
